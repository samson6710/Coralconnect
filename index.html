Coral Connect

Our device monitors and protects the coral ecosystem in order to help marine life. With CoralConnect, we can bring together people and the ocean through technology. Adopt a coral reef today to support our cause!

.01 Monitor

When someone adopts a coral, they can monitor it through our website. Users can see videos of the coral and observe its conditions in real time. We use LiDAR technology, a device that sends light and makes accurate maps of an area to make precise maps of the coral reefs twice a day. We plan to use AI and make an animated map of the corals according to the information we get.

.02 Analyze

After collecting the information from the device, it would be organized together with the data from past monitoring. These statistics would then be utilized to create the general trend of temperature in the coral reefâ€™s environment.

.03 Regulate

After collecting and analyzing information, CoralConnect would protect the coral reefs by providing a small area of a cooler/warmer temperature depending on the temperature trends. Even the slightest change could help make the environment more suitable for coral due to their sensitivity to habitat conditions.
